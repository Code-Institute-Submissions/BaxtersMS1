Our Services

Flu Vaccine Flu Vaccine: we provide flu vaccine administration to both adults and children. The flu vaccine is admistrated to children nasally and is available free to all children aged 2-12.
Blood Pressure Testing Blood Pressure Testing: We are pleased to offer a blood pressure measuring service. Simply call in and have your blood pressure checked if you are having any concerns. You are at higher risk if you smoke or are overweight. If you fall into one of these at rish groups, it is advised to have your blood pressure checked annually.
Blister Services Blister Services: We are happy to organise your prescription into a daily and weekly blister service for your convenience.
Diabetes Advice Diabetes Advice: If you have diabetes or think you might be at risk of developing diabetes, we can discuss your concerns and provide advice. Our trained staff can conduct a diabetes risk assessment, offer support and advice on blood glucose meters, lifestyle and diet.
Delivery Services Delivery Services: We are happy to deliver your prescription free of charge within a 10km radius of the shop.
Smoking Cessation Smoking Cessation: We in totalhealth Pharmacies have a specially designed Smoking Cessation programme that our Smoking Cessation Advisors follow. We use a combination of an initial consultation, agreeing a date, a pre-Quit consultation, first week support, follow up consultations to help our patients succeed.
